About ITL Logistics

Bridging complex logistics with seamless solutions since 2023

Our Story

Founded in 2023, ITL Logistics Egypt brings together a passionate team with over a decade of combined experience in international freight forwarding. We started with a simple mission: to bridge the gap between complex logistics challenges and seamless solutions.

What sets us apart is our deep understanding of the Egyptian market combined with our global network of partners. We've built relationships with carriers, customs authorities, and logistics providers worldwide, enabling us to offer comprehensive solutions that truly make a difference for our clients.

Today, we serve clients across diverse industries, from automotive manufacturers to pharmaceutical companies, helping them navigate the complexities of international trade with confidence and ease.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional freight forwarding services that empower Egyptian businesses to compete globally. We strive to be the bridge between our clients' cargo and its destination, ensuring every shipment arrives safely, on time, and within budget.

Our Vision

To become Egypt's most trusted freight forwarding partner, recognized for our reliability, innovation, and commitment to customer success. We aim to set new standards in logistics excellence while contributing to the growth of Egyptian trade.
